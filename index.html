YSleep

Problem:

At night, people sleep in closed bedrooms with poor ventilation. As we breathe, CO₂ levels can rise above 1500–2500 ppm, causing:

  • Poor sleep quality
  • Morning headaches
  • Lower cognitive performance
  • Increased risk for children and people with asthma
  • Allergens and dust remain trapped in the room

Solution:

Our device monitors air quality and automatically ventilates the room, ensuring fresh air while you sleep for better health and energy in the morning.
